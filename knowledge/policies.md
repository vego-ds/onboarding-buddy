# Company Onboarding Policies

## Access Policy

System access must be prepared according to the employee role and department. Sensitive or privileged access should not begin until the related approval is complete.

## Document Policy

Required employment documents should be collected before downstream onboarding work depends on verified identity, tax, or employment records.

## Approval Policy

Approval-required onboarding tasks must be reviewed before they move to active execution. Rejected or revision-requested approvals should be resolved before the task starts.

## Dependency Policy

Downstream onboarding tasks should not begin until their upstream dependencies are completed. Blocked tasks should remain paused until approval and dependency blockers are cleared.

## Timeline Policy

Important workflow events should be recorded so HR can audit onboarding progress, task status changes, approval decisions, and workflow execution history.
