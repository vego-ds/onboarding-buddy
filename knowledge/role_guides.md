# Role Guides

## Employee

Employees can ask about their onboarding tasks, blockers, required documents, orientation, policies, and next steps.

## Manager

Managers can ask about assigned employees, team onboarding readiness, buddy assignment, role-specific tools, and task blockers.

## HR

HR can ask about employee records, onboarding plans, approval queues, workflow timelines, and operational follow-up.

## IT

IT can ask about laptop preparation, account setup, tool access, and IT-owned onboarding tasks.

## Security

Security can ask about privileged access, approval requirements, blockers, and audit-related onboarding events.
