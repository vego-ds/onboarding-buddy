# Onboarding Handbook

## First Week Checklist

New employees should complete identity and employment documentation, attend orientation, review team expectations, confirm system access, and meet their onboarding buddy during the first week.

## Employee Responsibilities

Employees are responsible for completing assigned onboarding tasks, asking for help when blocked, and reviewing required company policies before accessing sensitive systems.

## Manager Responsibilities

Managers are responsible for confirming role expectations, assigning an onboarding buddy, reviewing team-specific tools, and making sure the employee has the context needed to begin productive work.

## HR Responsibilities

HR is responsible for employee records, document collection, onboarding plan generation, approval coordination, and lifecycle tracking.

## IT Responsibilities

IT is responsible for laptops, email accounts, system access, and role-specific tools.
